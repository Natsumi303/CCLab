Fizzy

Scientific Name:

Gyra Mutatus Fizzy

Discovered by / at:

Natsumi Zhang, Hongkong Disneyland, 10/20/2022

Family:

Fizzy belongs to the family Gyra. The members of this family share a common trait: they appear as floating, expressive faces, each with a distinct color tied to their emotional state. While Fizzy is known for its transformation from yellow happiness to red anger, others in its family show different emotional changes. Some may turn blue when sad, while others turn green when envious, etc. Besides, all Gyras have the ability to influence their environment to express their emotions, Fizzy is known to rotate objects as a response to emotional shifts.

Habitat:

The habitat of Fizzy is a cozy and intimate room filled with the energy of creativity and curiosity. The room is cluttered with a lot of everyday items, providing plenty of objects for the creature to interact with. When Fizzy grows angry, the confined space becomes a chaotic playground!!

Appearance:

In its normal state, Fizzy appear as a bright and yellow happy face, floating in the air. Its round with wide eyes and a joyful smile. However, when angered, its vibrant yellow body turns into a deep, intense red face. The entire face begins to shake violently

Key Characteristics:

  • Glides smoothly through the air
  • Becomes angy when not fed
  • Turns red and frowns when angry
  • Calm and helping when happy but causes chaos by rotating objects when angry

What we know so far about Fizzy? Fizzy is a small creature discovered in a vibrant garden at Hong Kong Disneyland. It resembles a yellow happy face that lives in a room. When calm and happy, it remains yellow and helps keep the room organized. However, if not fed, Fizzy gets angry, turning red. In this state, it moves faster and can rotate objects. Feeding Fizzy with a burger restores its calm state, and it resumes helping to organize the space.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature

  • Pay attention to Fizzy's color and expression. A bright yellow indicates happiness, while a red face signals anger.
  • Feed Fizzy every 5 hours, or immediately when it becomes angry
  • Spend time with Fizzy regularly
